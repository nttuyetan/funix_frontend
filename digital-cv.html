Goals

Goal 1
Increase the visibility of your professional profile and make your CV easily accessible to potential employers or collaborators.

Goal 2
Present your skills, experiences, and achievements in an engaging and dynamic manner to effectively showcase your strengths and capabilities.

Goal 3
Establish a strong personal brand and tailor your digital CV to reflect your unique identity, values, and career aspirations.

Scopes

IN

- Creating an interactive and visually appealing user interface (UI) for the digital CV.
- Developing features such as sections for personal information, work experience, education, skills, projects, and contact details.
- Allowing the user to easily input, edit, and update their CV content.
- Implementing features for adding descriptions, dates, and other relevant details for each section of the CV.
- Ensuring that the digital CV is accessible and displays correctly across various devices and screen sizes, including desktops, tablets, and mobile phones.
- Providing options to link and integrate the digital CV with the user's social media profiles (LinkedIn, GitHub, etc.) for enhanced visibility and networking opportunities.
- Offering customization features such as different templates, color schemes, fonts, and layout options to personalize the appearance of the digital CV.

OUT

- Managing the job application process, including job search functionality, application tracking, and job alerts.
- Providing features for submitting applications directly through the digital CV platform.
- Building features for networking, connecting with other professionals, or collaborating on projects within the digital CV platform.
- Incorporating features for scheduling interviews, sending messages, or conducting video interviews directly through the digital CV interface.
- Providing detailed analytics or reporting on user interactions, such as the number of views, downloads, or interactions with the digital CV.
- Offering personalized career advice, job recommendations, or matching services based on the user's profile and preferences.
